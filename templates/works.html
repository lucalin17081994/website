05 : Works

Ruby on Rails Project (2020)

In January 2020, I started working on a side project in Ruby on Rails. At the time, I was working at a fastfood restaurant, which had a delivery service.

I decided to build a website where customers could place orders to get delivered at their door. In about 2 months, I managed to finish this project, which you can find here: Github link

The website is full stack. Items can be added on the menu and customers can login and authenticate. Users can choose to order anonymously or authenticate and the orders can be printed out with the browser (chrome or firefox) from the restaurant side.

Due to management issues and the Coronavirus, the website was never deployed. However, I have gained a lot of experience with web development and the Rails framework.

Android Studio (2020)

Continuing my journey, I started a course on Android Studio. After learning about the basics, I started developing my own apps as hobby. Below are some of the apps I made in this period:

  • Pomodoro app
  • Todo app
  • Note taking app
  • Spendings tracker app

All above apps are connected to Google's Firebase functionality. This means that they all use google's authentication system and database.

The Pomodoro app is a productivity app based on the Pomodoro Technique. There is a 25:00 minute timer which can be started and stopped. Upon stopping or finishing, the app logs the time to the database. Previous work sessions can be viewed on the user page.
