04 : Starcraft II Coaching

StarCraft II: Legacy of the Void is a standalone expansion pack to the military science fiction real-time strategy game StarCraft II: Wings of Liberty, and the third and final part of the StarCraft II trilogy developed by Blizzard Entertainment. The game was released on November 10, 2015

Interested in learning more about this game or climb up the ladder? I offer coaching from €15 per hour. Some skills I can teach you:

  • Read And Execute Build Orders
  • Macro Management
  • Micro Management Training (splitting units, forming concaves, boxing method)
  • Hotkey Training (hotkey stealing, egg hotkeying, when you should use the all army hotkey)
  • Current Build Orders From Pro Level Games
  • Fun Ladder Builds

I can also analyze your replays and give you feedback on what you can improve on.

Make sure to join my discord and send me a message if you are interested!
